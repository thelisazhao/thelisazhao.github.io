Educational Videos Project

I think education should be shared. During my undergraduate and masters courses, I struggled a lot with learning complex math, theory and other skills. It wasn't just me that struggled, my classmates and friends did too. I wanted to make these math videos to help others when they're struggling.

In the end, people often give up not because of the content but the way its taught. Contact me if you have any suggestions of videos I should make. I'm learning as I go.
